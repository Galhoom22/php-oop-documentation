## Interview Question

What are traits, and how do you resolve trait method conflicts?

## What The Interviewer Is Testing

Whether you know PHP's code-reuse mechanism outside inheritance.

## A Strong Answer Should Cover

- A trait is a reusable bundle of methods inserted into a class.
- Traits help share behavior across unrelated classes.
- Conflicts can be resolved with `insteadof` and aliases can be created with `as`.

## Practice Task

Describe when a `Timestampable` trait is reasonable and when inheritance would be wrong.
